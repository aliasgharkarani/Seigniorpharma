Seignior Company Overview


Seignior Pharma is offering services as Manufacturer’s Representative and Marketers for world’s leading companies with following business segments:

a) Extensive pharma services like importing human & veterinary, medical and allied products
b) Importing, indenting, sales promotion, direct sales and marketing service as manufacturer companies representatives
c) Licensing and business development services
d) State-of-the-art Cold and Dry Storage facilities covering a wide area to accommodate bulk quality material within global and Pakistani standard and quality parameters.
c) Sales and distribution facilities providing services. Our highly experienced management team reviews business results and develops strategies aiming at long-term growth. The objective is to ensure objectivity in our business assessments, business opportunities and issues, and monitor our business results and related controls. These activities focus on: · Excellent customer services · Pioneering technology · Creativity · Integrity · Social responsibility

Seignior Core Values


Seignior Pharma ensures complete satisfaction to clients with its quality services. Since its launch on 25th of December 1992, the Seignior Pharma's values embodied in it corporate Credo reflect the vision of its founder and the President of the company Mr. Sher Afgan Khan at all levels. With years of extensive professional expertise in multi national and Pakistani pharmaceutical sectors, our team of professionals ensures these Credo values extend to our internal corporate structure a well as to its clientele and society through corporate social responsibilities. Seignior Pharma is responsible for:
i. Timely, accurate, reliable and objective business results by offering quality services of international standard for its international manufacturers and clients in Pakistan.
ii. Strong and effective corporate governance with ideal workplace approach.
iii. Continuously reviewing business results and strategic plans.
iv. Focus on maximum business stability through extended distribution, stockiest and sub-distributors network in all over Pakistan

Seignior Vision


Mr. Sher Afgan Khan

(President)

Mr. Sher Afgan Khan Founder prior to formation of this company headed various Multinationals in Pakistan and had a total experience of more than Five decades with Multinationals, Viz. Upjohn-USA, Siegfried, a company belongs to Switzerland and Asta Medica from Germany and Seignior Pharma. These companies were floated by him in Pakistan. After his last assignment with Asta Medica, Germany, he decided to start his own company as a Family Enterprise. He had his education in European Managed Schools, he was science graduate from the university and had taken Management Courses from the American Management Association, England, Switzerland, Belgium, Germany and Far East. He was a consultant & adviser to five Companies apart from his own company. After the Death of his beloved wife (A cancer patient for 25 years) He died in the same year 2014.
